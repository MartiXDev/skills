## Research pack template

### Purpose

Use this template when preparing a future research memo that feeds new rules,
maps, or a later expansion pass.

### Recommended outline

1. Scope and source priority
1. Candidate file inventory
1. Top-level quick-reference candidates
1. Deep-rule candidates
1. Official source list with descriptive links
1. Gaps, open questions, and later follow-up work

### Review prompts

- Which official docs are authoritative for the topic?
- Which parts belong in rules versus reference maps?
- Which items are first-pass ready and which remain backlog material?
- Which source exclusions still apply?
